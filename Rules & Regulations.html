Rules & Regulations

  • ‘’Shraddhaban Lavate Gnanam’’... Keeping these words in mind, students should maintain good conduct and politeness in school and outside.
  • The first and foremost duty of a student is to study. Formation of good character and sound mind in a sound body is the key foundation of good education.
  • Every student and his/her guardian should make an effort to uphold the reputation of the school.
  • Guardians are allowed to meet the Headmaster from 2 p.m to 4 p.m.
  • Every student must come to school in proper school uniform.
  • Every student must bring the school Diary on all working days. It is mandatory for all to attend prayer.
  • Students are forbidden to leave school without the permission of the Headmaster.
  • Every student should participate in co-curricular activities.
  • Every student should come to school daily. If he/she is absent, he/she must submit a leave application, countersigned by the guardian, to the class teacher.
  • 75% attendance per term is mandatory. Otherwise, he/she will be debarred from taking tests/exams.
  • Every guardian is requested not to send his/her wards if he/she is sick/afflicted by/exposed to infectious diseases.
